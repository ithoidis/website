Research Projects

Research on advanced signal processing algorithms for hearing aid applications.

Development of advanced techniques for audio semantic analysis and speaker recognition.

Development of audio signal processing and machine learning techniques for automated fault detection of industrial equipment, funded by Greece and the European Union (ESF).
