Research & Publications

Research Focus

My research focuses on hearing technology, aiming to restore speech perception for people with hearing loss in complex acoustic environments. My expertise includes deep learning, speech processing, audio semantic analysis, mobile audiometry, hearing aid design, and auditory modelling.

Selected Publications

  • Thoidis, I., & Delikaris-Manias, S. (2023). "Deep Learning Approaches for Speech Enhancement in Complex Acoustic Environments." IEEE Transactions on Audio, Speech, and Language Processing.
  • Thoidis, I., et al. (2022). "Mobile Audiometry: A Novel Approach for Hearing Assessment." Journal of the Acoustical Society of America.
  • Thoidis, I., & Delikaris-Manias, S. (2021). "Speaker Extraction in Noisy Environments Using Deep Neural Networks." IEEE International Conference on Acoustics, Speech and Signal Processing (ICASSP).
